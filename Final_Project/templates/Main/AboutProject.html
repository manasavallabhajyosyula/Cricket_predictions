Cricket Predictions

According to the survey results taken from a reliable source, cricket stands number 7 in terms of popularity across the world. The majority of participants of this sport come from Australia, India, Pakistan, South Africa and the UK, but cricket is also on the rise in other countries all over the world. Out of the love for cricket, people try to predict the outcome of a match and try to anticipate their favourite player statistics based on their individual cricket sense or based on luck strategy. Would it be wonderful if there is a prediction machine or a system which predicts statistics of a match in a more accurate way? That’s an obvious “YES”!

The prediction machine or system can be achieved through data science. This project requires chunks of previous data to predict and several algorithms to make prediction accordingly. This project can be used in prediction apps, fantasy games. It can also help in forming a suitable game strategy and improving player individual statiatics.

Our project can predict run rate of a team, strike rate of a batsman, economy of a bowler and number of wickets taken by a bowler for a specific match. From the above parameters, strike rate of a batsman, economy of a bowler, average of a batsman and average of a bowler can be used to predict most winning probable team.

Data

Data used in this project is substantially gathered from authenticate and legitimate source such as cricinfo.com. Our project deals with prediction of the above parameters for T20 format, so, the data extracted from the source are related to T20 format. It was later scrapped and visualized to confirm the data availability.

Predicted Parameters

This project can be used in prediction apps, fantasy games. It can also help in forming a suitable game strategy and improving player individual statiatics.

1. Runrate of Team:

It is known as runs per over i.e. number of runs scored in an over. KNN regressor is the algorithm used for finding the run rate.

2. Player stats in Match:

i. Strikerate:

It is defined for a batsman as the average number of runs scored per 100 balls faced. The higher the strike rate, the more effective a batsman is at scoring quickly. The algorithm used here to predict strike rate is gradient boosting regressor.

ii. Economy:

In cricket, a player’s economy rate is the average number of runs they have conceded per over bowled, the lower the economy, the better the bowler is. After testing with several algorithms, the algorithm suited for giving the most probable output is KNN regressor.

iii. Wickets:

Our project can also predict the number of wickets taken by a bowler in a match. The best algorithm suited to predict this kind of parameter is Random forest classifier.

3. Team:

This is the team which is given by our project model considering it the most probable. It gives out the team on the basis of 4 parameters respectively. Strike rate of a batsman, economy of a bowler, average of a batsman and average of a bowler is used. Here, averages are calculated where as strike rate and economy are predicted using gradient boosting and KNN regressor.
